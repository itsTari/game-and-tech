WELCOME TO GAME AND TECH FEST

ABOUT THE EVENT

Formally called Game and Tech Meetup, the Game and Tech Fest is an event created to introduce young people to the vast opportunities in Technology while having fun Gaming activities. The event offers a platform for Young people to learn practical in-demand digital skills and access Tech Scholarships, Startups to showcase their Products, and Small Business owners to learn about Technology for Businesses.

The maiden edition of the event which held in July 2020 introduced 110 Young people to 3D Printing & Scanning, Embedded Systems/Robotics, Programming and Game Development amidst fun gaming activities. We hosted the 2nd Edition of Game & Tech Meetup in November 2020 and introduced 120 Young people to Cybersecurity, Web Development, Embedded Systems/Robotics, Programming and Drone Flight/Operation. The event was an eye opener for these young people as they saw opportunities in Tech they could key into.
In November 2021, We hosted the 3rd Edition of Game & Tech Meetup and this time partnered with Microsoft Africa Development Centre, Microsoft Student Learn Community, and Gamers Zone to introduce 45 Young people to Microsoft Power Platform, Mixed Reality and AI gaming, 35 were introduced to Drone Flight/Operation, while 40 were introduced to other areas in technology and gaming. Amazing prizes were worn by the attendees to include Microsoft Branded Shirts, Bags and Notepads courtesy Microsoft ADC, as well as free vouchers to take any Microsoft Professional Exams.
The fourth Edition of the event will be holding in November 2022, and to expect its scope and give room for more attendance, it has been rebranded to the Game and Tech Fest
